About Us

BrightOut Foundation provide fast and effective support to people affected by disaster, refugee children and to fund education and other charitable initiatives. Also helps people to organize fundraisers for a dear cause, for someone or for an event. We span across several countries and currencies across the globe.

Our Beginning

Since 2019, we’ve worked with Dufil and some food industires to raise and distribute over $20 million shelter, clothing and feeding to people with immediate needs and organizations driving long-term change.

Our Mission

To empower a changemaker and community builders with easy-to-use tools so they can bring people together and make a difference.

Our Vision

Quickly distributing donations to on-the-ground aid organizations and people impacted by emergencies.

Our Mandate

A non-profit organization to harness the power of impact-driven philanthropic capital to address some of the world’s most urgent needs.

Our Plans

We are very excited to share that over 12 nonprofit organizations across over 19 states have benefited from the generosity of donations to the Community Fund and received grants so far. This project is particularly unique because its reach is wide, allowing for a deep impact at a community level. Together, we can uplift, support, and empower the Community to advance toward achieving a more equitable society.
